COVID-19 Mortality Trends: Asia vs. Europe

This analysis compares COVID-19 death tolls in the countries across Asia and Europe, highlighting distinct approaches and outcomes.

Key Observations

  • Asia: Early lockdowns and extensive testing contributed to generally lower death rates.
  • Europe: Initial waves hit harder, challenging healthcare systems and resulting in higher mortality rates in some countries.

Examining these trends reveals how regional policies and health infrastructure influenced the pandemic’s impact in each area.
